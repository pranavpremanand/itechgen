Engagement Models

Fixed Price

The Fixed Price model is structured into a defined timeline, a complete project scope, and a fixed budget

This type of engagement model is convenient for projects that aren't too complex, with an agreed upon time interval for a specified scope of work.

Time and Material

The T&M engagement model is structured into, number of man-hours, project scope, and an agile development methodology

This model is suitable for complicated and long-term projects with cross-functional teams. It provides flexibility to change the resources as needed.

Dedicated Team

We offer you our best dedicated development team based on the required skill set, competencies, and tech stack.

We will efficiently match the resources with your requirements once you share with us your business goals, number of resources, and technological preferences.
